About Me

Programming

I studied IT at university in Czech republic, but I decided it was the best option to quit school and immigrate to Canada in 2012. When I lost my Heavy Duty Technician position due to COVID-19 in March 2020, it motivated me to sign up for TECHCareers - Full Stack Developer course, carried by University of Alberta and get finally get back into what I started at University and what I'm strongly passionate about.

I've been teaching myself programming since 2005 on and off, picking up various languages along the way. Over the years I played with game modding tools and map creators, attempted to create a browser based strategy, learned how to work with Unity and most recently Unreal Engine 4, in which I've created my portfolio game Math Cubed using C++.
